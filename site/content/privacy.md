**Last updated:** March 2026

## Overview

Apno is a free, open-source apnea training app. Your privacy is important and this policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

Apno does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All training data (session history, settings, and preferences) is stored locally on your device in a SQLite database.

## Network Access

Apno does **not** require or use an internet connection. The app works entirely offline.

## Third-Party Services

Apno does **not** integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all associated data.

## Children's Privacy

Apno does not knowingly collect data from children. Since no data is collected from any user, there is no risk to children's privacy.

## Changes to This Policy

Updates to this policy will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/guillermodotn/apno).
