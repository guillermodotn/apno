Apno

Train your breath. Expand your limits.

A free, open-source apnea training app for freedivers, spearfishers, and anyone looking to improve their breath-hold capacity.

Get it on Google Play View Source

Training Programs

O2 Tables

Fixed hold time with decreasing rest periods. Trains your body to perform with less oxygen.

CO2 Tables

Increasing hold time with fixed rest periods. Builds tolerance to carbon dioxide buildup.

Free Training

Open-ended breath holds at your own pace. A simple timer with contraction tracking.

Progress Tracking

Session history with a heatmap calendar. See your training consistency at a glance.
